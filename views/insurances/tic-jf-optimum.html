Eligibility

To be eligible coverage under this plan, the applicant must:

  1. 1.be a visitor to Canada, a person with a Canadian work visa or super visa, an immigrant to Canada or a Canada resident, who is not eligible for a provincial or territorial government healthe insurance plan in Canada.
  2. 2.be at least 15 days on the date of purchase.
  3. 3.not have been diagnosed with a terminal illness; or not have been diagnosed with stage 3 ot 4 cancer; or
  4. 4.not have been diagnosed with or have required medical treatmentfor kidney disease requiring dialysis; or
  5. 5.not have been diagnosed with or have had an episode of congestive heart failure; or
  6. 6.not have had a lung condition for which, in the last 12 months, youhave been prescribed or used home oxygen; or
  7. 7.not have received or is awaiting a bone marrow or major organ transplant.
